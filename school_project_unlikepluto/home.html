Unlike pluto

Armond Arabshahi, known by his stage name Unlike Pluto, is an American singer, songwriter, DJ and record producer.
The name Unlike Pluto developed from holding onto his outcast persona.
He is known for his Pluto Tapes series of albums, where he challenges himself to release one song per week starting from May 2018.
