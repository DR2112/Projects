Jurassic World Evolution

A sequel to Jurassic World Evolution (2018) and set after Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom,

the game is set to be released for Windows,

PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One and Xbox Series X and Series S in November 9, 2021.
